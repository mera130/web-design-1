PIZZA

Pizzais a popular Italian dish made of a round, flat base of dough topped with ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese (usually mozzarella), vegetables, meats, and herbs. It is typically baked in an oven. Originating in Naples, Italy, pizza has become a global favorite, with many regional styles and variations like Margherita, Pepperoni, and deep-dish. It is often enjoyed as a casual meal or party food.
